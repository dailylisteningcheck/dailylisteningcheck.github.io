General Troubleshooting

  • Check for power

    • Is the processor turned on and in proper program? Check for visible signal light or LCD display. Replace batteries.

  • Check for visible defects

    • Are there frayed cables, cracked casing, or loose connections?

  • Check Transmitting Coil & Cables
    1. Confirm cables are plugged in correctly
    2. Hold signal check over transmitting coil when power is on (red light should illuminate)
    3. Replace transmitting coil one at a time and apply signal check after each component
    4. Check microphone is no transmission
